Homebrew & Homebrew Policy

I'm a big fan of Homebrew. I use it in every single game I run, and I feel that it helps to spice up and fill out the parts of a game that would otherwise be empty and hollow without it.

My only real rule for Homebrew is that you run it by me, and wait for a yes before you use it.

Homebrew made by others, be it from an unnoffical supplement, or just crafted by you or a friend, is allowed on a case-by-case basis. If I feel that something is unbalanced I do reserve the right to either take it away, nerf it, or buff it as nessecary for the health of the game.

Obviously if it's some of my own Homebrew, you're all good, seeing as I probably gave it to you. I also encourage you to create homebrew items yourself. It takes the burden off of me and, of course, I will give it a once or twice over with suggestions and concerns.

Unsolicited and Unapproved Homebrew is, frankly, what I would call a dick move. Don't do it. It's a violation of the trust and freedom I grant to you to make and use your own stuff when you want something new to play with.

On the subject of my own homebrew, you can also find my homebrew linked on the sidebar on the left.

I hope you enjoy using it as much as I enjoyed Making it.
