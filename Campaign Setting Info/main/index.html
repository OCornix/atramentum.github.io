Greetings Interloper

This campaign takes place in a larger setting. Many things have been lost to time, and many real events have become nothing more than myth, with the names of their ancient heroes and villains being completely forgotten. Anything you define for your character, so long as it does not explicitly violate the laws and continuity of this world, is true.

If your mother rode on the backs of dragons, it shall be so.

If your orphaned brother carved you from stone and, with the aid of a great mage, made you into a flesh and blood person, it shall be so.

If your father was murdered by a woman wielding one-half of a scissor-blade, it shall be so.

If you are a veteran past their prime, shaking off the rust of retirement, it shall be so.

You may, when making your character, invent entire nations, gods, and familial feuds.

And if you do not desire to invent an entire saga for yourself, you need not do so.

You only need a name, a reason to be here, and whatever specific requirements a campaign calls for.
